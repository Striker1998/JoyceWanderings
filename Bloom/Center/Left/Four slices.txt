She wants 3, no, 4 slices. She doesn't like her plate full. The pussens meows loudly at your ankles. You go and grab your bottle of Hanlon's milk and pour some into the dish. Some mutton kidneys? It is Thursday, not a good day at Buckley's. Pork kidneys at Dlugacz's might be good. Forgot the key in the trousers upstairs. Sneaking past the bedroom, you say softly in the bare hall "I'm going round the corner. Be back in a minute. You don't want anything for breakfast?" You hear a soft grunt come from Molly. No, she does not want anything. Grabbing your overcoat and hat, you ponder about your lost property houses. Leaving the house, you leave the door propped slightly open and step out into the warm summer Dublin streets.
